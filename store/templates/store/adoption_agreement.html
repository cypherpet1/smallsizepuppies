Health and Adoption Agreement

Please read and accept the following terms before adopting a puppy.

Health Guarantee

We guarantee that all puppies are in good health at the time of adoption. All puppies have been checked by a licensed veterinarian and have received their first round of vaccinations. We are not responsible for any health conditions that may arise after the adoption. It is the responsibility of the adopter to ensure proper veterinary care after the adoption.

Adoption Terms

By adopting a puppy from us, you agree to provide a loving and safe home. You agree not to breed, sell, or abandon the puppy at any time. You must ensure the puppy receives proper care, including adequate food, exercise, and medical attention. If you are unable to care for the puppy, we request that you return the puppy to us instead of surrendering them to a shelter.
